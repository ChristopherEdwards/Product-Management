NAME VA-IMMUNIZATIONS
PRINT NAME Immunizations
EDUCATIONAL OUTCOME 
The patient will recognize the importance of routine immunizations.
EDUCATIONAL STANDARDS 
1.  Review the recommended schedule for tetanus, pneumovax, and influenza
immunizations.
 
2.  Discuss the implications of not receiving the immunizations according to
schedule.
 
3.  Review VA and community resources available outside of the current
facility to obtain immunizations.
