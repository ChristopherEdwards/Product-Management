ABBREVIATED DESCRIPTION DIAGNOSTIC SUBSTANCE,KITS,SETS
DESCRIPTION 
IN VITRO DIAGNOSTIC SUBSTANCES,REAGENTS, TEST KITS AND SETS INCLUDES IN 
VITRO DIAGNOSTIC AIDS, SUCH AS TEST TABLETS, TEST STRIPS, TEST CARDS, TEST
PAPERS, TEST SOLUTIONS, REAGENTS; SETS AND KITS, DESIGNED FOR IN VITRO 
QUALITATIVE AND QUANTITIVE TESTS ON SPECIMENS, SUCH AD BLOOD, URINE, FECES,
AND THE LIKE, FOR THE PURPOSE OF AIDING IN THE MEDICAL DIAGNOSIS OF DISEASE,
BODY ORGAN DYSFUNCTION, OR OTHER ABNORMAL CONDITIONS. EXCLUDES IN VIVO
DIAGNOSTIC SUBSTANCES.
