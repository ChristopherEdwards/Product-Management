NAME LBRY PURGE ??
HEADER PURGE CHOICES
TEXT 
To PURGE one or more JOURNAL DATES from this title:
     1.   Enter a number displayed under the heading "ID NUM".
     2.   Enter a series of numbers displayed under the heading
          "ID NUM" separated by commas ( 1,2,3 ).
     3.   Enter a range of numbers separated with a dash ( 1-3 ).
     4.   Enter a combination of choices 2 and 3 ( 1,3-5 ).
 
"N" allows you to see (N)otes about this journal.
 
"B" will display the previous five journal dates.  You may enter
B("ID NUMN") to start at that journal date and see five journal dates.
 
"F" will display the next five journal dates.  You may enter F("ID NUM")
to start at that journal date and see five journal dates.
