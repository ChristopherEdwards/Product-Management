AN ELECTRONIC TRANSMITTER AND RECEIVERSYSTEM DESIGNED TO TRANSMIT,
RECEIVE, AND RECORD ECG DATA, FROM A PATIENT, TRANSMITTED VIA STANDARD
TELEPHONE AND/OR TELEPHONE LINES. TRANSMISSION MAY BE BY LONG DISTANCE
OR LOCAL TELEPHONE LINES. INCLUDED IN THIS SYSTEM ARE THE TRANSMITTER
WHICH IS LOCATED NEAR TEH PATIENT, THE RECEIVER WHICH IS LOCATED IN
DOCTOR'S OFFICE OR HOSPITAL AND IT MAY BE DE SIGNED TO RECEIVE ECG AND
PLUG INTO A RECORDER OR TO RECEIVE AND RECORD SIMULTANEOUSLY.
