HEADER TITLES DUE FOR RENEWAL
TEXT 
Titles Due For Renewal (DFR) lists those current subscriptions which
are due to be renewed.  The list is alphabetized by title and gives 
the following information:  ISSN, vendor, placement, renewal date,
length of the present subscription, and the number of copies.  The
list will be produced automatically; there are no sort prompts.
  
Please enter [DEVICE] to see how to print the list.
