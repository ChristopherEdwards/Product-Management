OIF OEF (10)    TEAM PURPOSE (403.47)

Name Value
TEAM PURPOSE OIF OEF
DESCRIPTION 
A non-Primary Care Team that is used specifically for OIF and OEF
Transition assistance. The information in PCMM related to the OIF/
OEF Team shall be transmitted to Austin in the same manner as
Primary Care Team information is transmitted. This information
will be monitored and tracked by the VSSC.
