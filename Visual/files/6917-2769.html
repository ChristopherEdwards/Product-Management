RECORDER, TIME. A MECHANICALLY OR ELECTRICALLY OPERATED CLOCK WHICH IS
PRIMARILY DESIGNED TO IMPRINT TIME DATA ON CARDS, DOCUMENTS, AND THE
LIKE. IT MAY BE EQUIPPED TO IMPRINT ADDITIONAL DATA NOT RELATED TO
TIME. IT MAY BE ACTIVATED MANUALLY OR AUTOMATICALLY BY CARD INSERTION.
IT INCORPORATES PROVISIONS FOR AUTOMATIC, SEMIAUTOMATIC, OR MANUAL
PRECISE POSITIONING OF LINED AND/OR COLUMNED "IN AND OUT" TIME CARDS,
"ELASPED TIME" JOB CARDS AND THE LIKE.
