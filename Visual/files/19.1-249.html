DESCRIPTION 
This key is for the Prosthetic Treatment Center administrative users.

The holder of this Key will also receive vital e-mail messages when a
purchasing transaction associated with the lab has been canceled, or
closed by either the Lab or the Administration Section. Since the receipt of
this mail is so critical, the EP has decided to deliver it based on this key
instead of relying on a Mail Group.  The mail is mandatory, and is not up
to the users to decide if they want it delivered or not.
