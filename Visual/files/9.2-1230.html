NAME FSC U906 CANCEL A CALL
TEXT 
 
                            Canceling a Call
 
Calls cannot be deleted.
 
If a call was made in error, it can be canceled.
 
To cancel a call, the status is changed to canceled.
 
A canceled call can be reopened by changing its status to open.
