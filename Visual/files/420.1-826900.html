826900 General Reference Laboratory (826900)    COST CENTER (420.1)

Name Value
NAME 826900 General Reference Laboratory
DESCRIPTION 
Contains the direct expenses of operating a CMD authorized reference 
laboratory. Also charged to this cost center are the expenses incurred 
in support of the Armed Forces Institute of Pathology (VA Medical Center, 
Washington, D.C. only).  Includes personal services, supplies, and 
purchased services.
