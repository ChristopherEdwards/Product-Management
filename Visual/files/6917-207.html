WASHER-EXTRACTOR-CONDITIONER, CONTINUOUS FLOW, LAUNDRY, AUTOMATIC. A
LAUNDRY SYSTEM CONSISTING OF A WASHER, EXTRACTOR, AND CONDITIONING
TUMBLER. SOILED WASH IS DROPPED ONTO A CONVEYOR BELT AND CONTINUOUSLY
FED TO A WASHER, AUTOMATICALLY FLOWS THROUGH ALL STEPS OF THE
LAUNDERING FORMULA AND OUT TO ANOTHER CONVEYOR BELT TO A WATER PRESS
EXTRACTOR TO REMOVE EXCESS WATER. THEN ONTO AN ASCENDING CONVEYOR BELT
TO GO INTO AND THROUGHA CONDITIONING TUMBLER. THE CLEAN DAMPWORK IS
SHAKEN-OUT AND DROPS OUT FOR FLATWORK IRONING, PRESSING OR ROUGH DRYING
AND FOLDING. PROCESSES UP TO 1,650 LBS. OF CLOTHS PER HOUR.
