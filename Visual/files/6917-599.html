PUMP, RECIPROCATING. A POSITIVE DISPLACEMENT DEVICE WHICH UTILIZES THE
RECIPROCATING MOTION OF ONE OR MORE PISTONS, PLUNGERS, OR DIAPHRAGMS

LIQUID FROM ONE PLACE TO ANOTHER. IT IS DESIGNED TO BE DRIVEN MANUALLY
OR BY A PRIME MOVER WHICH IS NOT AN INTEGRAL PART OF THE PUMP.
