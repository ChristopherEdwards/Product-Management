VA-AAA SCREENING NOT APPLICABLE (53)    REMINDER TERM (811.5)

Name Value
NAME VA-AAA SCREENING NOT APPLICABLE
DESCRIPTION 
This term is for diagnoses and health factors that represent reasons that 
screening for AAA is not likely to be useful or appropriate.  Sites 
should consider adding any local health factors for terminal illness or 
short life expectancy to this term.
FINDINGS
  1. FINDING ITEM:   IMAGING FOR AAA N/A CURRENTLY
    BEGINNING DATE/TIME:   T-6M
  2. FINDING ITEM:   IMAGING FOR AAA N/A PERMANENTLY
  3. FINDING ITEM:   VA-TERMINAL CANCER PATIENTS
    BEGINNING DATE/TIME:   T-6M
CLASS NATIONAL
SPONSOR VA National Center for Health Promotion and Disease Prevention (NCP)
