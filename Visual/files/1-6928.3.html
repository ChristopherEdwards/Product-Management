ENG BUILDING (6928.3)    FILE (1)

Name Value
NAME ENG BUILDING
DESCRIPTION 
A simple file of physical buildings. A 'division designation' may
be included if needed to distinguish between two buildings having
the same number. Suppose, for example, there were two buildings
numbered 100 at VAMC St. Louis, one at the John Cochran Division
and one at Jefferson Barracks. These two buildings could be entered
separately as 100-JC and 100-JB; where 'JB' and 'JC' are division
designators. The same strategy may be useful at facilities that
support outpatient clinics, Regional Offices, national cemeteries,
etc.
