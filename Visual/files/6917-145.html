BRIEF DESCRIPTION DESOLDER TOOL ELEC
NOMENCLATURE 
DESOLDERING TOOL, ELECTRIC. AN ITEM DESIGNED TO TRANSMIT HEAT FROM AN
ELECTRIC HEATING ELEMENT TO A DESOLDERING TIP/HEAD FOR DESOLDERING ONE
OR MORE CONNECTION(S) OR MULTIPLE-POLE COMPONENTS PARTS FROM
WIRINGBOARDS. DURING THE DESOLDERING PROCESS, THE CONNECTION(S) OR
JOINT(S) ARE HEATED; WHEN MULTIPLE, HEATED SIMULTANEOUSLY. THE HOLES
ARE FREED BY SUCTION FROM SOLDERING MATERIAL SO THAT THE PART MAY BE
REMOVED AND REPLACED BY A NEW ONE. IT MAY INCLUDE A HAN DLE, A SUCTION
APPARATUS, A RELEASE MECHANISM, A HEATING HEAD, A DESOLDERING TIP, AN
EXTRACTING DEVICE, A POWER SUPPLY, AND A CASE.
