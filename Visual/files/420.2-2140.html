NAME 2140 Commercial Transportation Charges
DESCRIPTION 
INCLUDES RENTAL OF PASSENGER CARS;CHARTER OF TRAINS, BUSES, VESSELS OR AIRPLANES AND EXPENSES [INCIDENTAL TO THE] OPERATION OF RENTED OR CHARTERED CONVEYANCES. (CHARGEABLE TO LIMITATION .007.)
