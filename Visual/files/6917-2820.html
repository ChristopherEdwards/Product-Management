ANALYZER, OPTICAL, MULTICHANNEL. A DEVICE DESIGNED TO DETECT, STORE,
QUANTITATIVELY PROCESS, DISPLAY AND SIGNAL AVERAGE OPTICAL DATA FROM
MANY CHANNELS SIMULTANEOUSLY, IN REAL TIME. IT CONVERTS LIGHT SIGNALS
INTOELECTRICAL SIGNALS WHICH ARE THEN ANALYZED FOR LIGHT ABSORPTION,
INTENSITY AND WAVELENGTH. FOR USE IN SUCH APPLICATIONS AS ASTRONOMY,
KINETIC CHEMISTRY, LASER RAMAN INSTRUMENTS, LIGHT SOURCE/PHOSPHOR
SPECTRA, MASS AND ELECTRON SPECTROSCOPY, ULTRAVIOLET, VISI BLE, AND
INFRARED ABSORPTION, AND ULTRA-CENTRIFUGE ANALYSIS. ITUSUALLY INCLUDES
A TELEVISION CAMERA TUBE, DIGITAL PRINTER OR COMPUTER, OSCILLOSCOPE AND
X-Y PLOTTER.
