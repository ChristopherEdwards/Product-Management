CONTROLS THE RATE OF SAMPLING AND SAMPLE/WASH RATIO. IT MONITORS EACH
OF THE ANALYTICAL CHANNELS IN PRESCRIBED SEQUENCE AND CONDITIONS THE

PRINTED ON A RECORDER CHART. THE VALVES ARE CLOSED AT PRE-SET, PERIODIC
INTERVALS, AND A REGENERANT BUFFER AND 50% METHYL CELLOSOLVE ARE PUMPED
THROUGH THE APPROPRIATE SYSTEMS. IT MAY INCLUDE THE NECESSARY MODULAR
EQUIPMENT.
