ABBREVIATED DESCRIPTION AIR CONDITIONING EQUIPMENT
DESCRIPTION 
NOTE:  THIS CLASS INCLUDES ONLY COMPLETE AIR CONDITIONING EQUIPMENT,
SYSTEMS AND PLANTS.  ALSO INCLUDED ARE SPECIALLY DESIGNED NONOPERATING
ACCESSORIES SUCH AS WINDOW PANELS USED WITH A WINDOW AIR CONDITIONER TO
FACILITATE MOUNTING.  EXCLUDED ARE OPERATING COMPONENTS, ASSEMBLIES,
PARTS, ATTACHMENTS, OR ACCESSORIES FOR USE IN OR ON AIR CONDITIONING
EQUIPMENT, WHICH ARE TO BE CLASSIFIED IN FSC 4130.
INCLUDES:  STORE TYPE UNITS, WINDOW AND ROOM TYPE UNITS, MARINE AND
VEHICULAR AIR CONDITIONING UNITS, AIR CONDITIONING PLANTS AND UNITS.
EXCLUDES:  AIR CONDITIONING COMPONENTS (FSC 4130).
