KEYBOARD ACTUATED) UNIT OF A COMPUTER SYSTEM WHICH PUNCHES COMPUTED
RESULTS INTO CARDS, READS INPUT INFORMATION AND SEGREGATES CARDS. THE
READ-PUNCH UNIT GENERALLY CONSISTS OF A CARD FEED, A READ STATION, AND
