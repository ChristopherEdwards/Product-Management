DEFINITION 
An organization that provides prosthetic and orthotic care which may 
include, but is not limited to, patient evaluation, prosthesis or 
orthosis design, fabrication, fitting and modification to treat limb loss 
for purposes of restoring physiological function and/or cosmesis or to 
treat a neuromusculoskeletal disorder or acquired condition.
Source: American Board for Certification in Orthotics, Prosthetics, and 
Pedorthics, Inc.
