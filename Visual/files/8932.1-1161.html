Name Value
PROVIDER TYPE Transportation Services
CLASSIFICATION Air Carrier
STATUS Active
X12 CODE 344800000X
DEFINITION 
An air company that the Federal Aviation Administration, the 
certificate-holding district office (CHDO), regional Flight Standards 
Division (RFSD) offices, and AFS-900 has verified that the company is 
capable of operating safely and that it complies with the regulations and 
standards prescribed by the Administrator.
Source: Federal Aviation Administration
