METER, PHOTOGRAPHIC EXPOSURE. AN INSTRUMENT WHICH MEASURES THE QUANTITY
OF LIGHT REFLECTED FROM OR ON A SUBJECT IN EXPOSURE UNITS BY MEANS OF A
PHOTOELECTRIC CELL. THIS FIGURE, WHEN USED IN CONJUNCTION WITH AN
INTEGRAL TABLE OR A CALCULATOR, GIVES A SUITABLE EXPOSURE TIME AND LENS
APERTURE FOR THE EMULSION SPEED OF THE NEGATIVE MATERIAL USED. IT MAY
INCLUDE CASE, STRAP AND/OR HANDLE AND OTHER ACCESSORIES.
