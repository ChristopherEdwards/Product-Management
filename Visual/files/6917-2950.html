BRIEF DESCRIPTION CONT OXYGEN LVL ENVIR
NOMENCLATURE 
CONTROLLER, OXYGEN LEVEL, ENVIRONMENTAL. AN ITEM WHICH CONTINUOUSLY
MONITORS THE LEVEL OF OXYGEN IN AN ENCLOSED CHAMBER IN ORDER TO
MAINTAIN A SPECIFIED LEVEL OF OXYGEN. IF THE OXYGEN LEVEL GOES
OVER/UNDER THE PRE-SET PERCENTAGE, THE DEVICE RESPONDS BY INFUSING
ENOUGH NITROGEN/OXYGEN INTO THE ENVIRONMENT TO MAINTAIN THE APPROPRIATE
OXYGEN LEVEL.
