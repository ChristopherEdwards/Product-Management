Name Value
NAME VA-SUBSTANCE ABUSE MEDICATIONS
PRINT NAME Substance Abuse Medications
EDUCATIONAL OUTCOME 
If applicable, the patient and family will recognize the importance of
complying with a prescribed medication regimen.
EDUCATIONAL STANDARDS 
1.  Review the mechanism of action of the prescribed medication.
 
2.  Discuss the side effects of prescribed medications.
 
3.  Emphasize the importance of not self-medicating for any purpose.
 
4.  Review OTC medications (e.g., cough syrup) that contain ETOH/drug additives
and the signs/symptoms of innocent ingestion.
