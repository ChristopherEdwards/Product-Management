NAME PRCHPM PO REC
MENU TEXT Receipt of Purchase Order
UPPERCASE MENU TEXT RECEIPT OF PURCHASE ORDER
DESCRIPTION 
This function is used by A&MM to update the receipt of an order.
The service may indicate receipt of partial orders.  Subsequent
partials will show the amount of goods received on previous deliveries.
