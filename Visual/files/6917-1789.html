BRIEF DESCRIPTION RADI THER PLAN SYST
NOMENCLATURE 
RADIATION THERAPY PLANNING SYSTEM. A MODULAR TREATMENT PLANNING PROGRAM
SYSTEM WHICH IS CAPABLE OF CALCULATING AND COMBINING IONIZING RADIATION
DOSES GIVEN TO A PATIENT IN CLINICAL FORMATS REQUIRED IN THE
ONOCOLOGICAL MANAGEMENT OF MALIGNANT DISEASES. SYSTEM INCLUDES A DATA
MANAGEMENT SYSTEM OF GENERALIZED PROGRAMS FOR THE COLLECTION, STORAGE
AND ANALYSIS OF DATA, COMPUTER AIDED DOSIMETRY SYSTEM FOR BEAM
EVALUATION, ANALYSIS, AND ENTRY THROUGH EITH ER A FILM ISODENSITOMETER
OR A WATER PHANTOM, RECORD AND VERIFY SYSTEM TO VERIFY CORRECT PATIENT
SET-UP ON TELETHERAPY UNITS AND TO RECORD THE TREATMENTS GIVEN.
