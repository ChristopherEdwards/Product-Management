BRIEF DESCRIPTION RADIO GAS DEL SYSTEM
NOMENCLATURE 
RADIOACTIVE GAS DELIVERY SYSTEM, PULMONARY. AN APPARATUS DESIGNED TO
DISTINGUISH PULMONARY EMBOLISM DISEASE FROM OTHER PERFUSION
ABNORMALITIES THROUGH THE INJECTION, TO THE PATIENT, OF A LOW DOSE OF
SUCH RADIOACTIVE GASES AS XENON, KRYPTON AND OXYGEN. THE RADIOACTIVE
INJECTION TRACES THE COURSE OF THE DISEASE, WHICH IS THEN LOCATED BY A
SCANNER. IT MAY BE SELF-CONTAINED AND CONSIST OF AN ANALOG DISPLAY, CO2
ABSORBER, RECIRCULATING FAN AND BELLOW TYPE SPIROMETER S.
