SOLDERING AND BRAZING OUTFIT, RESISTANCE HEATING. MAKE AND MODEL A/A.
AN ITEM CONSISTING OF A POWER UNIT WITH SOLDERING (OR BRAZING) LEADS
AND ATTACHMENTS. THE POWER UNIT CONVERTS A HIGH VOLTAGE-LOW CURRENT
SOURCE INTO A LOW VOLTAGE-HIGH CURRENT OUTPUT. IN OPERATION, THE OUTPUT
CURRENT FLOWS THROUGH AN ELECTRODE OF THE ATTACHMENT, WHICH RAISES THE
TEMPERATURE OF THE METAL WITH WHICH IT IS IN CONTACT, BY RESISTANCE
HEATING, UNTIL THE MELTING POINT OF THE SOLD ER OR BRAZING ALLOY IS
REACHED.
