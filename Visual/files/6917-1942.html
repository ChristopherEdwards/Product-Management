MONITOR, PRESSURE RELIEVING, BED AND WHEELCHAIR PATIENT. AN ITEM
DESIGNED TO HELP ELIMINATE THE PROBLEM OF DECUBITUS ULCERS OF PATIENTS
CONFINED TO A WHEELCHAIR OR BED. THE PATIENT IS MONITORED BY A BATTERY
OPERATED UNIT CONNECTED TO THE WHEELCHAIR OR BED. AN ALARM IS ACTIVATED
AT PRE-SET TIMED INTERVALS INDICATING THAT THE PATIENT MUST MOVE OR BE
MOVED IN A MANNER TO PREVENT DECUBITUS ULCERS.
