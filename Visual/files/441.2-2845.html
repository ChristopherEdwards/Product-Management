Name Value
NUMBER 2845
ABBREVIATED DESCRIPTION ROCKET ENG. COM.
DESCRIPTION 
ROCKET ENGINES & COMPONENTS
INCLUDES:  ROCKET PRIME MOVERS, LIQUID TYPE, INCLUDING LIQUID JATO UNITS, 
FOR USE IN AIRCRAFT, ROCKETS, AND GUIDED MISSILES; LIQUID PROPELLANTS
ENCASED IN CONSUMABLE CONTAINERS, INTENDED FOR INSERTION INTO MISSILE
PROPULSION SYSTEMS AS INTEGRAL PARTS.
EXCLUDES:  ROCKET MOTORS (SOLID PROPELLANT UNITS); LIQUID PROPELLANTS 
PREDETERMINED TO SPECIFIC QUANTITY AND QUALITY, BUT PACKAGED IN REUSABLE
CONTAINERS; SOLID PROPELLANT SQUIBS AND CARTRIDGES, WHETHER INTENDED 
FOR INSERTION INTO LIQUID PROPELLANT SYSTEMS OR NOT.
