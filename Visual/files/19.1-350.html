DESCRIPTION 
Patient claims for Nose and Throat Radium Treatment status as
part of the Veterans Millenium Health Care and Benefits Act,
Public Law 106-177, Section 20 must be verified using either
the patient's military medical record or military service
record.  This key allows access to the Verification questions in
Field 17 of Screen 6 in the LOAD/EDIT PATIENT DATA option and
controls edit access to Field 17 after claim verification is
complete.
