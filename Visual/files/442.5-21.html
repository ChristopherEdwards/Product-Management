NAME 1358 OBLIGATION
ABBREVIATION 1358

DESCRIPTION 
The original 1358 form is entered by a service.  Fiscal then obligates
for the entire estimated 1358 amount.  As the service receives charges
from the vendor against the 1358, they are entered, then at the end of
each month, an adjustment is made by Fiscal for the actual amount
charged against the 1358.  No processing takes place in Supply.
