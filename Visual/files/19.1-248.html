DESCRIPTION 
The holder of this Key is designated as a Prosthetic Treatment Center
Lab Supervisor and may review and complete the 2529-3 lab requests.
 
The holder of this Key will also receive vital e-mail messages when a
purchasing transaction associated with the lab has been canceled, or 
closed by either the Lab or the Administration Section. Since the receipt
of this mail is so critical, the EP has decided to deliver it based on
this key instead of relying on a Mail Group.  The mail is mandatory, and
is not up to the users to decide if they want it delivered or not.
