TEXT 
A choice of 1, 2, or 3 will also allow a change in the DATE RECEIVED
field to be visible in History of Check-In (HIS) for this
copy, but this change will not be visible on the main Check-In
(CHE) screen.
  
The @ delete mark will not work in the DATE RECEIVED field.  Instead,
use choice 4--NOT RECEIVED-- under ROUTING STATUS to indicate
non-receipt of a copy of an issue.
  
If all copies of an issue are marked NOT RECEIVED, the "received"
column of the main check-in screen will be emptied.  If appropriate,
the issue may then be checked-in again with a new DATE RECEIVED,
and the DATE RECEIVED column on the main screen in Check-In (CHE)
will be accurate.
