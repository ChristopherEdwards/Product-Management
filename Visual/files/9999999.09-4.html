NAME VA-SUBSTANCE ABUSE COMPLICATIONS
PRINT NAME Substance Abuse Complications
EDUCATIONAL OUTCOME 
The patient and family will explain how to avoid the complications of
chemical dependency and slow the progression of the disease by compliance
with a prescribed daily program.
EDUCATIONAL STANDARDS 
1.  Review the effects that the different chemicals have on the body. 
Emphasize the long term effects of the patients continued use of the
patients specific dependency.
 
2.  Discuss the symptoms indicative of progression of disease.
 
3.  Review the effects of chemical dependency on all family members.
