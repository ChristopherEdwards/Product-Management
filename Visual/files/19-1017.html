LRAC A AC AR (1017)    OPTION (19)

Name Value
NAME LRAC A AC AR
MENU TEXT Mumps A, AC, & AR indexes of the LAB REPORTS file
UPPERCASE MENU TEXT MUMPS A, AC, & AR INDEXES OF T
DESCRIPTION 
This option does a re-cross-reference on the "A" and "AR" indexes of 
the LAB TEST subfield and the "AC" index of the TEST LOCATION subfield
within the LAB REPORTS FILE.  All three indexes are re-cross-referenced.
