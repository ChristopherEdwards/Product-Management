DEFINITION 
An emergency medicine physician who specializes in non-hospital based 
emergency medical services (e.g., disaster site, accident scene, 
transport vehicle, etc.) to provide pre-hospital assessment, treatment, 
and transport patients.
Source: National Uniform Claim Committee, 2009
 
Additional Resources: A Certification of Added Qualifications (CAQ) is 
issued by the American Osteopathic Board of Emergency Medicine.
