INDIVIDUALS-OTHER  Any person supplying an item or a service that is not
                   a Department of Veterans Affairs employee.
   
OTHER COUNTRIES  Any country supplying items or services other than
                 the United States of America.
   
COMMERCIAL-RECURRING PMTS  Any non-government source that will supply goods
                           or services on a repetitive basis.
   
UTILITY COMPANIES  Any company regulated by local or federal government.
   
VETERANS  Anyone that served in the armed services.
   
CANTEEN  The store in a Department of Veterans Affairs facility.
