BRIEF DESCRIPTION REFRACTOMETER (LAB)
NOMENCLATURE 
REFRACTOMETER (LABORATORY). AN INSTRUMENT FOR DETERMING THE REFRACTIVE
INDEX OF A SUBSTANCE, BY MEANS OF A RAY OF LIGHT PASSING OBLIQUELY FROM
ONE MEDIUM INTO ANOTHER OF DIFFERENT DENSITY, OR BY THE CHANGE IN
DIRECTION OF THE FLOW OF AN ELECTRIC CURRENT WHEN IT PASSES FROM ONE
MEDIUM TO ANOTHER OR DIFFERENT CONDUCTIVITY OR OF OTHER DIFFERING
ELECTRIC QUALITIES. IT MAY BE CAPABLE OF DISPENSING THE SOLUTION SAMPLE
INTO CENTRIFUGE TUBES FOR FURTHER TESTING.
