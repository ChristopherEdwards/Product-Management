NAME 4110 Grants, Subsidies and Contributions
DESCRIPTION 
Includes grants and payments to States for research purposes, and for
care and treatment of beneficiaries at State homes; readjustment benefit
payments and readjustment allowances; loan guaranty payments in lieu of
interest; miscellaneous benefit payments for specially adapted housing
; payments under 38 U.S.C. ch. 35.
