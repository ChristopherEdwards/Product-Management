BRIEF DESCRIPTION ENTOPTOSCOPE
NOMENCLATURE 
ENTOPTOSCOPE. AN INSTRUMENT FOR EXAMINING AND OBSERVING THE INTERIOR OF
THE EYE TO EVALUATE AND DIAGNOSE DISEASE THAT AFFECTS THE MACULA,
INCLUDING MASCULAR DEGENERATION, DIABETIC MACULOPATHY, CENTRAL SERIOUS
RETINOPATHY, GLAUCOMA, AND VASCULAR OCCLUSIONS. IT CONSISTS OF A BLUE
LIGHT SOURCE, AN OPTICAL SYSTEM WHICHEMITS THE BLUE LIGHT, AND A
CROSSHAIR FOR FIXATION AND LOCALIZATION OF ABNORMAL AREAS OF
PERCEPTION.
