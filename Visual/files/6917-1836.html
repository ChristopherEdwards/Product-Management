BRIEF DESCRIPTION TRANS CASE RAD FILM
NOMENCLATURE 
TRANSPORT CASE, RADIOGRAPHIC FILM, DAYLIGHT TYPE. A UNIT DESIGNED FOR
USE IN A SEMI-AUTOMATIC RADIOGRAPHIC FILM PROCESSING SYSTEM. IT MOUNTS
ON AN UNLOADER, RADIOGRAPHIC FILM CASSETTES, DAYLIGHT TYPE DEVICE,
WHERE ITS FUNCTION IS TO RECEIVE AND STORE QUANTITIES OF FILM IN
VARIOUS SIZES. IT IS THEN TAKEN TO THE DARKROOM,WHERE THE FILMS ARE PUT
INTO AN UNLOADING TRAY AND PROCESSED.
