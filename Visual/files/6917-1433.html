BRIEF DESCRIPTION LITHOTRIPTOR
NOMENCLATURE 
LITHOTRIPTER. AN ELECTRONIC DEVICE DESIGNED TO DISINTEGRATE BLADDER OR
KIDNEY STONES BY MEANS OF ULTRASONIC OR ELECTROHYDRAULIC SHOCK WAVES.
STONE FRAGMENTS CAN THEN BE REMOVED BY SUCTION. IT CONSISTS OF AN
ULTRASONIC OR ELECTROHYDRAULIC GENERATOR, FOOTSWITCH, AND CONNECTING
CABLE. THE CABLE IS INSERTED INTO THE BLADDER OR KIDNEY THROUGH THE
OPERATING SHEATH OF A CYSTOSCOPE AND/OR URETERO-RENOSCOPE.
