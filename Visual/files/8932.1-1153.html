DEFINITION 
Health educators work in a variety of settings providing education to 
individuals or groups of individuals on healthy behaviors, wellness, and 
health-related topics with the goal of preventing diseases and health 
problems. Health educators generally require a bachelor's degree and may 
receive additional training, such as through mentoring, internships, or 
volunteer work.
