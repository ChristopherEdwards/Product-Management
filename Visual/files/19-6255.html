NAME ONCO FOLL-PATIENT INQUIRY
MENU TEXT Patient Follow-up Inquiry
UPPERCASE MENU TEXT PATIENT FOLLOW-UP INQUIRY
DESCRIPTION 
On-line basic information is made available to the Tumor Registrar
after selecting a patient. This option is most useful upon receipt
of phone-calls by other Hospitals/Tumor registrars attempting follow-up
of patients they may be treating or following.
