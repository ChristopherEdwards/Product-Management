BRIEF DESCRIPTION OCULAR FLUOROPHOTOMET
NOMENCLATURE 
OCULAR FLUOROPHOTOMETER SYSTEM. A COMPUTERIZED SYSTEM FOR MEASURING THE
CONCENTRATION PROFILE OF THE TRACER FLUORESCEIN WITHIN THE EYE, AND FOR
PROCESSING THE DATA INTO AN EASILY ACCESSIBLE FORMAT. THE DATA IS USED
TO MEASURE BLOOD-RETINAL BARRIER FUNCTION, AND BLOOD-AQUEOUS BARRIER
FUNCTION, AND TO COMPUTE AQUEOUS FLOW. MAJOR COMPONENTS OF THE SYSTEM
INCLUDE: THE OPTIC HEAD, COMPUTER, DISPLAY MONITOR, AND PRINTER.
