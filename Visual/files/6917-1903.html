GENERATOR, OXYGEN, PATIENT CARE, MOBILE. UNION CARBIDE CORP. AN
APPARATUS DESIGNED TO PRODUCE OXYGEN, UTILIZING A PHYSICAL MEANS FOR
EXTRACTING THE GAS FROM THE ATMOSPHERIC AIR. IT SEPARATES THE NITROGEN
FROM THE OXYGEN IN THE AIR AND THE NITROGEN IS DISCHARGED AS A WASTE.
USED TO PROVIDE EMERGENCY OXYGEN FOR PATIENT TREATMENT. MAY BE USED IN
INHALATION THERAPY AREAS AND HOME PATIENT CARE PROGRAMS.
