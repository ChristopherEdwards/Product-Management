LOCATOR, RADIOGRAPHIC, OCULAR FOREIGN BODY. A DEVICE FOR LOCATING SOLID
PARTICLES IN THE EYEBALL BY MEANS OFROENTGENOGRAMS WITH DIFFERENT X-RAY
TUBE POSITIONS AND EMPLOYING PREPARED CHARTS TO LOCATE THE POSITION OF
THE FOREIGN BODY WITH RESPECT TO THE EYE. IT CONSISTS OF A HEADREST AND
CASSETTE TUNNEL, HEAD ATTACHMENTS, EAR CUSHION, CASSETTE PAN, INDICATOR
AND CHARTS. IT IS DESIGNED FOR USE WITH X-RAY APPARATUS, RADIOGRAPHIC.
