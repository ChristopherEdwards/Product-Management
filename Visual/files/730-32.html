GENERAL PRIVILEGE 
A.  Qualified to diagnose, treat, and manage patients with problems in
customary, and reasonable methods and techniques, including restraint,
seclusion and pharmacotherapy.
 
B.  In emergency situation, perform procedures dictated by the
emergency, even though not herein specified.
 
C.  When functioning in the Outpatient, Admissions and Ambulatory Care
areas or as Officer-of-the-Day (O.D.), perform the primary and secondary
care functions for a physician in the Outpatient Section.
 
D.  Admit and discharge patients; complete a history and physical
examination.
