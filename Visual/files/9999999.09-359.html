NAME VA-DIABETES FOOT CARE
PRINT NAME Diabetes Foot Care
EDUCATIONAL OUTCOME 
The patient will recognize the importance of daily foot care in preventing
complications.
EDUCATIONAL STANDARDS 
1.  Explain the importance of daily foot checks.  Explain that loss of
sensation leads to callouses.  Callouses break down, ulcers result which,
unchecked, may lead to gangrene and amputation.
 
2.  Stress the importance of daily foot hygiene.  Warm (NOT HOT) bathing of the
feet, complete drying, and thorough inspection for changes in the condition of
the skin constitute daily foot care.  Demonstrate how to do a thorough foot
check.
 
3.  Emphasize the use of clean, non-restrictive socks/stockings and
well-fitting shoes.
 
4.  Stress the importance of immediate follow-up of any foot injuries or
ulcers.  The patient should be non-weight bearing, whenever there are lesions on
the foot, to prevent further cellular damage.
