TEXT 
To interpret the above list of selections use the following legend:
 
  A '**' before an entry means the entry has been previously selected
         on this patient's care plan.  On certain terminals, the
         entry will also be highlighted.
 
  A '+' before an entry means that another list of choices exists for
        this entry.  Thus if this entry is chosen, another list of
        selections will appear to further define this entry.
 
  When text appears within an entry surrounded by square brackets:
    1. If the entry was previously selected, this text was entered to
       further clarify the entry.
    2. If the entry was not previously selected, then what is inside will
       be used as a default value.
