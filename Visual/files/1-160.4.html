RECONSTRUCTIVE SURGERY (160.4)    FILE (1)

Name Value
NAME RECONSTRUCTIVE SURGERY
DESCRIPTION 
RECONSTRUCTIVE SURGERY reconstructs, restores, or improves the shape and
appearance or function of body structures that are missing, defective,
damaged or misshapen by cancer or cancer-directed therapies.
Reconstruction may be a part of the first course of therapy or may be
done after the first course is completed.
