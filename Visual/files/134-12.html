NAME DEDICATED ROOM

DESCRIPTION 
A OPERATING ROOM (NOT OTHERWISE INDICATED BY OTHER O.R. TYPE CODES) THAT
IS EQUIPPED FOR GENERAL ANESTHESIA AND USED ONLY FOR A RESTRICTED SET OF
SURGICAL/DIAGNOSTIC PROCEDURES AT THE EXCLUSION OF ALL OTHERS, EXCEPT
UNDER EXTREME CIRCUMSTANCES.
