FOR THE DUMPING OF BEDDING MATERIALS SUCH AS HARD WOOD CHIPS, DANDER,

DESIGNED TO DRAW ANY ALLERGEN AND OTHER CONTAMINANTS INTO A FILTERING
SYSTEM THROUGH A BLOWER AND THEREBY MINIMIZING AIR TURBULENCE IN THE
WORK AREA. MAY BE CON NECTED TO A ROOM EXHAUST VENTILATION SYSTEM.
