This class includes only complete drones.  End items, assemblies, parts,
atachments, or accessories for use in or on drones are classified in
classes other than this class.  Includes Drones sepcifically designed
for such uses as targets, training, surveillance, missile evaluation,
and photographic reconnaissance and OA&MM SIUG membership positions.
