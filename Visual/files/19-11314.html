NAME SD MH NO SHOW AD HOC REPORT
MENU TEXT High Risk MH No-Show Adhoc Report
UPPERCASE MENU TEXT HIGH RISK MH NO-SHOW ADHOC REP
DESCRIPTION 
    This report will list the patients who have the patient record flag for 
High Risk for suicide and No Show an appointment in a mental health or non
mental health clinic.
    The report will list the patient, emergency contacts,next of kin,future 
scheduled appointments. provider and results.
    Mental Health clinics are face-to-face Mental Health appointments 
defined in the "VA-MH NO SHOW APPT CLINICS LL" Reminder Location List.
