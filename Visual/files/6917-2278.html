SCALER-CALCULATOR, VIDEO IMAGE DIMENSIONS. AN ELECTRONIC DEVICE
DESIGNED TO MEASURE THE DIMENSIONS OF OBJECTS IN A TV PICTURE BY
CALCULATING THE DISTANCE BETWEEN TWO LINES APPEARING ON THE DISPLAY. A
GIVEN DISTANCE BETWEEN TWO LINES IS CALIBRATED AND SERVES AS A SCALE
AGAINST WHICH OTHER DISTANCES ARE MEASURED. AN ENHANCER SHARPENS UP THE
PICTURE FOR MORE PERCISE MEASUREMENT.
