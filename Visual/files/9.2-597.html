In FileMan terminology editing means changing or altering data already
entered.  It does not mean deleting data altogether, leaving the field
empty.  There are some places where data may be either EDITED or DELETED;
there are other places where data may be EDITED only, not DELETED.  The
system will tell you which actions are appropriate.
