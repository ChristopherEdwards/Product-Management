Name Value
NAME RADIATION TREATMENT VOLUME
DESCRIPTION 
This item may be used as a quality assurance monitor to evaluate the
overall pattern of care within a facility.  It is potentially useful
as a monitor of appropriateness and efficacy of treatment and in
selecting patients for outcome reporting.

In many cases, RADIATION TREATMENT VOLUME will be most appropriately
coded by the radiation oncologist.
