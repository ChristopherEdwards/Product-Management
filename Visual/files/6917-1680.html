MANDIBLE MANIPULATOR, ELECTRONIC, DENTAL. AN ELECTRONIC INSTRUMENT
DESIGNED TO ASSIST THE DENTIST IN MANIPULATION OF A PATIENT'S JAW BY
MEANS OF ELECTRONIC CONTROLS AND ELECTRICAL IMPULSES. IT ELIMINATES THE
NEED FOR VOLUNTARY CLOSURE AND OPENING OF THE MANDIBLE BY THE PATIENT.
IT ACTUATES THE MUSCLES TO CLOSE ANDOPEN THE MANDIBLE THUS IMPRESSIONS
ARE TAKEN AUTOMATICALLY. MAY BE WITH OR WITHOUT ACCESSORIES, SUCH AS
ELECTRODES, ETC.
