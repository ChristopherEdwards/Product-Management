NAME VA-DIABETES LIFESTYLE ADAPTATIONS
PRINT NAME Diabetes Lifestyle Adaptations
EDUCATIONAL OUTCOME 
The patient will identify the lifestyle adaptations that are necessary to
reduce the effects of diabetes.
EDUCATIONAL STANDARDS 
1.  Review necessary modifications in diet, exercise, weight control, daily
medication regimen, health surveillance, and an awareness for prompt follow-up
of illness and infection using the diabetic protocols for diet, exercise,
follow-up, and medications.
 
2.  Discuss the methods of blood glucose control-weight control, oral
hypoglycemics, and insulin.
 
3.  Assess the patients's lifestyle and review areas needing improvement. 
Stress the individual's responsibility in controlling diabetes.
