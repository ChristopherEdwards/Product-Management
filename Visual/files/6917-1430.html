LEG-GAGE, ARTIFICIAL LIMB FABRICATING. AN ADJUSTABLE ITEM DESIGNED TO
PROPERLY MEASURE A PATIENT FOR A COMPLETE ARTIFICIAL LEG OR BELOW KNEE

PATIENT WALKS, THE ITEM MAY BE ADJUSTED TO MEET EVERY PHASE AND ANGLE
FOR PROPER ALIGNMENT. ITEM IS THEN PLACED ON A JIG AND PERMANENT
ARTIFICIAL LIMB IS DUPLICATED.
