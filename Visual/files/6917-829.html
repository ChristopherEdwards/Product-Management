SUCH THINGS AS LENGTH OF TIME OF A TELEPHONE CALL, LENGTH OF TIME A

ESTABLISHING THE MOST ECONOMICAL TELEPHONE SERVICE LEVEL AND
DETERMINING ABUSE OF TELEPHONES BY PERSONNEL. IT MAY BE INTERFACED WITH
A COMPUTER.
